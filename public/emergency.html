First Aid Guides
💔
Cardiac Arrest / CPR
Step-by-step CPR instructions for adults
1
Call emergency services (911) immediately.
2
Place heel of hand on center of chest. Interlock fingers.
3
Push hard and fast — 100–120 compressions/min, 2 inches deep.
4
After 30 compressions: tilt head, lift chin, give 2 rescue breaths.
5
Continue until help arrives or AED is available.
🫁
Choking (Adult)
Heimlich maneuver for airway obstruction
1
Ask "Are you choking?" If they can't speak, act immediately.
2
Stand behind them, wrap arms around waist.
3
Make a fist above navel. Cover with other hand.
4
Give 5 firm upward abdominal thrusts.
5
Repeat until object is expelled or person loses consciousness.
🩸
Severe Bleeding
Controlling major hemorrhage
1
Call emergency services if bleeding is severe.
2
Apply firm pressure with clean cloth or bandage.
3
Do not remove cloth — add more on top if soaked.
4
Elevate injured area above heart level if possible.
5
Apply tourniquet above wound if bleeding doesn't stop.
🔥
Burns
First response for thermal burns
1
Move away from the heat source immediately.
2
Cool burn with cool (not cold/ice) running water for 20 min.
3
Remove jewelry/tight items near the burn.
4
Cover loosely with non-fluffy sterile bandage.
5
Seek medical help for large, deep, or facial burns.
⚡
Stroke (FAST)
Recognize and respond to stroke symptoms
F
Face — Ask them to smile. Is one side drooping?
A
Arms — Can they raise both arms? Does one drift down?
S
Speech — Is speech slurred or strange?
T
Time — Call 911 immediately. Note the time symptoms started.
🤯
Seizures
Protecting someone during a seizure
1
Stay calm. Note the time the seizure started.
2
Clear hard or sharp objects from the area.
3
Cushion their head. Turn them on their side.
4
Never restrain them or put anything in their mouth.
5
Call 911 if seizure lasts 5+ minutes or person is injured.
