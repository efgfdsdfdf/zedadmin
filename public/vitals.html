📖 Clinical Reference Ranges
❤️ Heart Rate
● Normal: 60–100 bpm
● Low: <60 bpm (bradycardia)
● High: >100 bpm (tachycardia)
● Danger: >150 or <40 bpm
🩸 Blood Pressure
● Optimal: <120/80 mmHg
● Normal: <130/85 mmHg
● Elevated: 130–139/85–89
● High: ≥140/90 mmHg
🌡️ Temperature
● Normal: 97.6–99.0°F
● Low-grade fever: 99.1–100.4°F
● Fever: >100.4°F
● Hypothermia: <96°F
💧 SpO₂
● Excellent: 97–100%
● Normal: 95–96%
● Low: 92–94%
● Critical: <92%
